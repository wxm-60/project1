Food

Food in Hangzhou is characterized by its sweet and light flavors, enjoyable presentation, and delicate cooking methods. The most famous Hangzhou dishes include West Lake fish in sweet and sour sauce, shelled shrimp with Dragon Well tea, and Dongpo pork.

Famous food in Hangzhou

  • Sweet and Sour West Lake Fish (西湖醋鱼)
  • Dongpo Pork (东坡肉)
  • Fried Shrimp with Longjing Tea (龙井虾仁)
  • Aunt Song Fish Broth (宋嫂鱼羹)
  • West Lake Lotus Root Powder (西湖藕粉)
  • Victory Cake (定胜糕）
